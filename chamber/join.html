Membership Levels & Benefits

Our Chamber offers four membership levels. Each level provides different benefits, so choose the one that best fits your needs and organization type.

NP Membership

This membership is for non-profit organizations. It's free and provides access to several valuable benefits that will help your organization grow.

  • Access to special events and workshops
  • Networking opportunities with other businesses and non-profits
  • Discounted advertising rates on the Chamber website

Bronze Membership

For just NGN50 per year, the Bronze Membership offers a solid starting point for businesses looking to expand their exposure and resources.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Spotlight advertising on the Chamber website
  • Exclusive access to training programs to enhance business skills

Silver Membership

At NGN100 per year, the Silver Membership includes all the benefits of the Bronze Membership, with added features to give your business even more visibility.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Discounted rates for Chamber-hosted events and conferences
  • Feature placement on the Chamber homepage for 1 month

Gold Membership

For the highest level of membership at NGN200 per year, Gold Membership provides premium benefits and exclusive opportunities for your business.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Priority placement at special events
  • Exclusive email newsletters with industry insights and news
